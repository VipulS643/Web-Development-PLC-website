About Us

This is the Placement Cell of Pratibha College

Welcome to our Placement Cell! We are a dedicated team of professionals who are committed to helping students achieve their career goals. Our mission is to provide the best possible support and guidance to our students in securing meaningful employment opportunities. At our Placement Cell, we work closely with both students and companies to bridge the gap between academia and industry. We provide a range of services, including career counseling, job search assistance, resume writing, interview preparation, and more. Our team has a wealth of experience in the recruitment industry, and we leverage our network of industry contacts to connect students with top companies in their field of interest. We also organize various career-related events, such as guest lectures, workshops, and job fairs, to help students develop the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the workplace.
